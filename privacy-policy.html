Effective date: August 08, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Frogshut LLC (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our mobile application Paws N' Jars (the “Game”).

You may have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. To make such requests, contact us at the email below.
